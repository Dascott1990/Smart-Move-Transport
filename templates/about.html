About Us

Capitol City Contracting

Ottawa's trusted contracting partner, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology

Our Story

Founded in Ottawa with a vision to transform the contracting industry, Capitol City Contracting brings together decades of construction expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results for our clients.

As part of Dascott Global Ventures, we leverage innovative project management tools and communication platforms to ensure every project runs smoothly, on time, and within budget.

Our Mission

To provide Ottawa homeowners and businesses with reliable, tech-enabled contracting services that simplify the renovation process and deliver outstanding quality.

Our Vision

To become Ottawa's most trusted contracting company by consistently exceeding expectations and building lasting relationships with our clients.

Our Team
