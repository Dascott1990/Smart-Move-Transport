Our Story

SmartMove Transport was founded with a simple mission: make moving stress-free, transparent, and professional. What began as a small Ottawa-based moving service quickly grew into one of the region’s most trusted transport and delivery partners.

Backed by Dascott Global Ventures, we combine efficiency, technology, and customer-first service to deliver smooth moves, same-day deliveries, and reliable transport — all with clear communication and fair pricing.

Our Mission

To make every move simple, fast, and stress-free through reliable movers, modern booking technology, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To become Ottawa’s top-rated moving & transport company through professionalism, speed, and absolute dedication to customer satisfaction.
